Frequently Asked Questions

What should I bring to the hammam?

You don't need to bring anything. Peştemal (towel wrap), scrub mitt, natural soap and all materials are provided by our facility.

Is the hammam suitable for first-timers?

Absolutely! Our therapists explain the process step by step and adjust the treatment for your comfort. The traditional body scrub & foam is ideal for a first experience.

What should I do after the hammam?

We recommend drinking plenty of water and avoiding sun exposure for 2-3 hours. Since your skin will be renewed, use SPF protection.
