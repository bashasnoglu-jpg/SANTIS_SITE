THE STORY

The Santis Story

Santis Club is a wellness centre in the heart of Antalya that blends traditional Ottoman hammam culture with a modern luxury spa philosophy. With our "Quiet Luxury" approach, we offer each guest a personalised ritual of purification and renewal.

A sanctuary on the shores of the Adriatic in Montenegro, harmonising centuries-old spa traditions with contemporary touches, dedicated to holistic well-being of body and soul.

Santis Club is not merely a spa — it is a philosophy of living. Guided by the principle of "Health Through Water" (Salus Per Aquam — SPA), our spaces are designed to offer guests an atmosphere where time slows down, far from the rush of everyday life.

Our Philosophy

For us, luxury is not about extravagant décor — it is about serenity, purity, and attentive care. Our "Quiet Luxury" philosophy places simplicity and quality at the forefront of every detail. From the oils we use to the texture of every towel, everything has been meticulously selected to awaken your senses.

Our Location

Positioned against the breathtaking panorama of the Bečići coastline, our centre reflects the healing power of nature in its very architecture. Accompanied by the scent of the sea and the song of the birds, you are invited to renew yourself with the masterful touch of our expert therapists.
